Population density in Himachal Pradesh, India.

Himachal Prased is a northen state in India.90% of the state's population is employed in agriculture (census 2011). This choropleth map illustrate the population density present in each district of Himachal Pradesh. Kullu and Hamirpur are the most densely populated districts.

Land Cover data in Himachal Pradesh.

This bivariate map illustrate the percentage of agriculture and forest cover in each district of Himachal Pradesh, India. The data is sourced from Land Use Statistics, 2018. Maximum agricultural land is found in Mandi, Una, Shimla and Hamirpur. Maximum forest cover is seen in Chamba, Bilaspur, Kullu, Lahul&Spiti.

Percentage of educational attainment in St.Louis County, Minnesota.
